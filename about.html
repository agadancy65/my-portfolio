My Tech Journey So Far

What I Have Done

My journey into tech began with a deep curiosity about how websites and digital platforms work. Over time, I transformed that curiosity into action by diving into core areas of computer science. I completed several foundational courses such as Introduction to Programming, Computer Fundamentals, and Communication in English, all of which helped me develop logical thinking and effective communication—two key skills in tech.

I have hands-on experience building fully functional websites using HTML and CSS. From creating clean layouts to responsive designs, I’ve been able to bring concepts to life on the web. I’ve also explored and practiced with popular website builders like WordPress and Wix, where I designed and customized pages for both personal and client projects.

One of my most exciting accomplishments is learning how to set up and manage online stores using platforms like Shopify. I’ve helped businesses launch their e-commerce sites, working on product listings, custom themes, and basic SEO setups. I also use Canva and other design tools to create attractive digital materials, like restaurant menus and product labels, which I now offer professionally on platforms like Fiverr.

Beyond coding, I’ve participated in class projects and technical presentations where I explained how certain programs work, wrote live code during tests, and answered challenging questions from instructors and classmates. These activities sharpened my confidence, technical explanation, and quick problem-solving.
